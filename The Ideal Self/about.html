About Ideal Self

The ideal self is a project that allows the user to build a virtual character that is the ideal image of themself. It is an idealized version of yourself created out of what you have learned from your life experience, the demandds of society, and what you admire in your role models. On this website there series of interestes, personalityies and skills for user to choose from. The user can pick them based on what they have already possessed and what they wish to possess. After finishing the building of their ideal self, user can talk to them face to face like on video chat. Also there are more advanced interests, personalities and skills to purchase if the user want a complex ideal self.
For more information, please contact us by idealself@gmail.com. We hope you enjoy this website.
